About us

Welcome to First Door, a premier export company based in Egypt, founded in 2023 by our esteemed Executive Director, Ali Hassan.

First Door is a high-quality delivery company, proudly registered with the Ministry of Investments and Property in Egypt. We specialize in opening the gateway to Egypt's finest products for markets around the globe.

Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Hassan, we excel in the export of high-quality agricultural produce, textiles, and industrial materials. We are committed to delivering excellence with every shipment, ensuring that our clients receive only the best.

Trust First Door to be your reliable partner in bringing the richness of Egypt to the world.
